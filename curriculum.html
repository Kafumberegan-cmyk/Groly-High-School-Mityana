Ordinary Level

Ordinary Level Curriculum

We teach a variety of subjects at Ordinary Level which are chosen to mold our students in an all-round manner and prepare them for any A-level combination whether sciences or arts and can apply for any professional course at University and institutions of higher learning and successfully get enrolled. The subjects we offer keep changing with time as we review our students' needs and abilities. We have embraced hands on training. This is spear headed by our Principal and headteachers of the Campuses.

Advanced Level

Advanced Level Curriculum

As one of the top schools for Government admissions each year, we offer a diverse range of A-level subjects designed to shape well-rounded students, preparing them for any combination—sciences or arts—and ensuring they qualify for professional courses at universities and other institutions. Our subjects evolve to meet the needs and strengths of our students, and we've embraced hands-on training. The Principal oversees supervision and upholds educational standards across campuses to ensure quality compliance.

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Math
  • Geography
  • History
  • CRE / Divinity
  • Technical Drawing
  • French
  • Luganda
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Literature
  • Fine Art
  • Economics

Career Guidance

We place great emphasis on career guidance to help students choose competitive courses. Each campus has a Head of Careers Department who runs various programs to assist students in selecting the right subjects that align with their interests.
