Below is the approved fees structure for the 2026 academic year. All fees are payable per term unless stated otherwise.

Important Notes

  • Fees are payable per term and are non-refundable after two weeks of the term.
  • All bank charges are borne by the payer.
  • Day scholars' fees include lunch and afternoon tea.
  • Boarding fees include: accommodation, meals (breakfast, lunch, supper), and evening tea.
  • Development fund of UGX 50,000 per year is included in the fees.
  • PTA fees of UGX 20,000 per term are included.
  • Examination fees are charged separately for candidate classes (S.4 & S.6).
