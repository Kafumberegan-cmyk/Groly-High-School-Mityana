General Admission Requirements

Age Requirements

  • S.1: 13-15 years
  • S.2: 14-16 years
  • S.3: 15-17 years
  • S.4: 16-18 years
  • S.5: 17-19 years

Academic Documents

  • PLE Results Slip (for S.1)
  • UCE Results Slip (for S.5)
  • Last School Report
  • Transfer Letter
  • Leaving Certificate

Personal Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport Photos (4 copies)
  • Immunization Card
  • National ID (if available)
  • Parent/Guardian ID

O-Level (Senior 1 - 4) Requirements

For Senior One Admission

PLE Results:

Minimum aggregate of 12 in best four subjects including English and Mathematics.

Division Requirements:
  • Division 1: Direct admission (Aggregate 4-12)
  • Division 2: Interview required (Aggregate 13-24)
  • Division 3: Entrance exam required (Aggregate 25-28)
English Proficiency:

Minimum grade of 5 in English language.

Mathematics:

Minimum grade of 5 in Mathematics.

Students transferring from other schools at S.2, S.3, or S.4 must provide transfer letters and sit for placement exams.

Available Subject Combinations (S.1-S.4)

Sciences

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics

Arts

History, Geography, CRE, Literature

Languages

English, French, Kiswahili, Luganda

Vocational

Entrepreneurship, ICT, Art & Design

A-Level (Senior 5 - 6) Requirements

For Senior Five Admission

UCE Results:

Minimum of 5 passes including English and Mathematics.

Science Combinations:

At least a credit in the subjects you wish to take at A-Level.

  • BCM: Credit in Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • PCM: Credit in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • PCB: Credit in Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • MCB: Credit in Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology
Arts Combinations:

At least a credit in the subjects you wish to take at A-Level.

  • HED: Credit in History, Economics, Divinity
  • LEG: Credit in Literature, Economics, Geography
  • HEG: Credit in History, Economics, Geography
  • FEG: Credit in French, Economics, Geography
Minimum Requirements:
  • Division I or II in UCE
  • At least 5 credits in relevant subjects
  • Pass in English and Mathematics

Selection for competitive combinations (BCM, PCM) is based on merit and subject grades.
