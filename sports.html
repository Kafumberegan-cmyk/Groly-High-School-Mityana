Sports at Glory High School

Excellence in Athletics • Building Champions • Team Spirit

Football (Soccer)

What to know:

  • Participants: Boys & Girls
  • Age Group: (13-20) YRS
  • Competitions: District, Regional & National

About Football

We take part in the football/soccer sport and have been remarkably excellent at it in the past years at the district, regional and national Copa Coca Cola competitions having won trophies at district in the last five years, regional and the 2017 Copa Coca Cola trophy! We engage in both boys and girls football.

About Netball

We also have netball as a sport and have been excellent at it in the past years at the district, regional and national level won trophies & accolades at district in the last five years. This sport is also available as both a part of the Physical Education (P.E) program and as a competitive sport.
