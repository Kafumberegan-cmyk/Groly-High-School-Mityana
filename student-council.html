Student Council

Voice of the Students • Leaders of Tomorrow • Serving with Integrity

Welcome from the Student Council

The Glory High School Student Council is the representative body elected by students to voice their concerns, organize events, and foster a positive school environment. We work closely with the school administration to ensure that every student's voice is heard and respected.

Our council is dedicated to serving the student community, promoting leadership skills, and creating memorable experiences throughout the academic year.

Council Structure

Head Prefect

Overall leader of the student body, represents students in school administration meetings.

Deputy Head Prefect

Assists the Head Prefect and takes charge in their absence.

Academic Prefect

Oversees academic affairs and coordinates study programs.

Disciplinary Prefect

Maintains discipline and ensures school rules are followed.

Sports Prefect

Organizes sports activities and inter-house competitions.

Entertainment Prefect

Plans school events, talent shows, and entertainment activities.

Welfare Prefect

Looks after student welfare and addresses concerns.

Chapel Prefect

Coordinates religious activities and chapel services.

Health & Environment Prefect

Ensures cleanliness and promotes environmental awareness.

Our Responsibilities

Student Voice

Represent student concerns and suggestions to the school administration during monthly meetings.

Event Planning

Organize school events including Freshers' Ball, Talent Shows, and End of Year Parties.

Discipline

Work with teachers to maintain discipline and promote positive behavior among students.

Peer Support

Provide support and guidance to fellow students facing academic or personal challenges.

School Improvement

Initiate projects to improve school facilities and environment for all students.

Community Service

Organize outreach programs to give back to the local community.
