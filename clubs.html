Clubs & Societies

Discover Your Passion • Make Friends • Build Skills

Beyond the Classroom

At Glory High School, we believe education extends beyond textbooks. Our clubs and societies provide students with opportunities to explore interests, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships.

Every student is encouraged to join at least one club. With over 15 active clubs, there's something for everyone!

How to Join a Club

All students are welcome to join any club of their choice. Club activities are held after school hours and on weekends.

1

Choose Your Club

Review the list of clubs and select one that matches your interests.

2

Meet the Patron

Speak with the club patron during the designated meeting time.

3

Register

Fill out the registration form and pay the annual club subscription fee.

4

Start Participating

Attend meetings regularly and participate in club activities.

Annual club subscription: UGX 5,000 per club
