Our Academic Departments

Glory High School is organized into specialized academic departments, each led by experienced Heads of Department (HODs) who ensure curriculum delivery meets the highest standards. Our departments work collaboratively to provide students with a comprehensive and integrated education.

Science Department

The Science Department is equipped with modern laboratories and staffed by dedicated teachers who inspire curiosity and scientific inquiry. We offer Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics at both O and A Level.

Subjects Offered:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics

Humanities Department

The Humanities Department fosters critical thinking, cultural awareness, and understanding of society. Our teachers use interactive methods to bring history, geography, and religious education to life.

Subjects Offered:

  • Geography
  • History
  • CRE / Divinity
  • Islamic Studies
Head of Department:

Ms. Sarah Nakato - MA History, 12 years experience

Languages Department

Communication is key to success. Our Languages Department develops proficiency in both local and international languages, enabling students to express themselves effectively and appreciate diverse cultures.

Subjects Offered:

  • English
  • French
  • Kiswahili
  • Luganda
  • Literature

Vocational & Technical Department

  • Entrepreneurship
  • ICT
  • Art and Design
  • Technical Drawing
  • Physical Education
