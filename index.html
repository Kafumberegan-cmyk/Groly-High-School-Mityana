WELCOME TO GLORY HIGH SCHOOL

Welcome to Glory High School, an institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic student development. Our mission is to nurture young minds, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed in today's fast-changing world. With a team of passionate educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant learning environment, we are committed to fostering both personal and academic growth.

Since our first school opened in 2000, we have expanded into a network of four campuses—Main, Mukono, Green, and A-Level—focused on developing innovative leaders and well-rounded students. We now also offer Nursery and Junior sections (www.seetajnrsch.net), ensuring a complete learning cycle, while delivering exceptional educational experiences.

I look forward to partnering with you to provide your child with the best education and support throughout their time with us.
