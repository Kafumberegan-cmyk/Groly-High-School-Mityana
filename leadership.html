School Leadership

This is the team spearheading the progress of the mighty school.

Director Seeta Schools

Mrs. Rose Namayanja Muyingo

It is a great honor for me to address you on behalf of Glory high schools. We are focused on preparing children for the changing conditions of today's and tomorrow's worlds.

If we want our children to take part in the competition of the 21st century, and take the lead, they should have such qualities as self-confidence, assertiveness, leadership and all other values we stand for. As parents, our utmost responsibility is to provide an educational environment for our children for classwork and talent development.

Simply offering a competitive curriculum is not enough, thus, only with well-developed interpersonal and social skills and a sense of ethics will our children truly be equipped for the challenges they will face as the leaders of the future. With that goal in mind, welcome to Seeta High schools.

Principal Seeta Schools

Mr. Kafumbe Davis

Welcome to the Seeta High School website. The purpose of this platform is to provide parents, students and the community with easy access to a wealth of information on our school programmes and activities. As a school, our primary goal is to mould God fearing, academically excellent and responsible leaders.

For more than 20 years, we have and are still unconditionally committed to providing the best to our students. Our staff always focuses on developing a holistic personality with the right knowledge ready to take on the challenges head on once they step out of the gates of the school. We believe that education is a lifelong process which creates an understanding of moral and ethical values to guide one's life in a right path.

Seeta high school is a family of four secondary schools namely; Seeta high school Main campus, Seeta high school Mukono campus, Seeta high school Green campus and Seeta high school A campus. Besides, we are proud of three junior schools whose staff is passionate about the instilling of the right values to the pupils and provision of an excellent all round education in a state of art facilities.

You are welcome to the family.
