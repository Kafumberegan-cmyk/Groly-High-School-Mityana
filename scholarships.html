Investing in Bright Futures

At Glory High School, we believe that every deserving student should have access to quality education regardless of their financial circumstances. Our scholarship and bursary programs are designed to recognize excellence, support talent, and assist those in need. We offer a variety of opportunities for both new and continuing students.

Orphan Support Program

Full Scholarship

For orphaned students with proven academic potential and financial need.

Requirements:
  • Death certificates of parents
  • Recommendation from LC1
  • Minimum aggregate of 20 in PLE

Vulnerable Child Bursary

Partial Scholarship

  • Income assessment

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarships, but you will only be awarded the highest value scholarship for which you qualify.

When are scholarship deadlines?

Most scholarships close between August and October for the following academic year. Check each scholarship for specific dates.

Are scholarships renewable?

Yes, most scholarships are renewable annually based on maintaining good academic standing and conduct.

Do I need to submit separate applications?

Yes, scholarship applications are separate from the general admission application. Both must be completed.
