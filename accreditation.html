Our Accreditation

Recognized for Excellence • Certified for Quality • Committed to Standards

Officially Accredited & Recognized

Glory High School holds full accreditation from the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) and is registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports. Our commitment to educational excellence is validated through multiple certifications and recognitions.

Our Accreditations & Memberships

UNEB Registration

Fully registered and recognized by the Uganda National Examinations Board to conduct national examinations at both O and A Level.

Reg. No: UNEB/123/2020

Ministry of Education

Registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports, Uganda. Fully compliant with all national educational standards and guidelines.

Reg. No: MoES/456/2019

ISO 9001:2015

Internationally recognized quality management systems certification for educational institutions.

Cert. No: ISO/789/2021

Association of International Schools

Member of the Association of International Schools in Africa (AISA) and the Uganda Private Schools Association.

Member Since 2015
